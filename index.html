Common Questions

Why FY2025 and not an average?
FY2023 includes a one-off $124K grant and Higher Ground was half its current size. Averaging in those years sets an artificially easy target. FY2025 reflects the business as it stands today.
What about Higher Ground's reimbursements?
Reimbursements ($63K-$209K/yr) are pass-through costs. They have been excluded from all figures. Higher Ground revenue is commissions only, giving a cleaner picture of actual earned income.
Should we update the baseline once FY2026 closes?
Yes. FY2026 ends 31 March. Once final figures are in, confirm whether FY2025 or FY2026 is the fairer starting point before the hire's performance period begins.
Is 10% all-or-nothing too risky?
Revenue swings year to year. A tiered approach (5% = $10K, 10% = $20K) would reward incremental growth and keep motivation through slower periods. Worth discussing.
